Meet Yara

I obtained my Master’s degree in Nutrition at the Lebanese American University which helped me broaden my knowledge in the health and wellness sector and discover that the key to a healthy lifestyle is balance, variety, and moderation.

I also believe that a healthy lifestyle should include exercise, and that’s why I became a Zumba and Mobility instructor. Why? Fitness and exercise can improve your brain health, boost your metabolism, help manage weight, reduce stress and boost your mood, reduce the risk of disease, and improve your overall health.
