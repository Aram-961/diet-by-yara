Meet Yara

She obtained her Master’s degree in Nutrition
at the Lebanese American University which helped her broaden her knowledge in the health and wellness sector and discover that the key to a healthy lifestyle is balance, variety, and moderation.

She also believes that a healthy lifestyle should include exercise, and that’s why she became a Zumba instructor. Why Zumba? The combination of dance and exercise can improve your brain health, boost your metabolism, help manage weight, reduce stress and boost your mood, reduce the risk of disease, and improve your overall health.
